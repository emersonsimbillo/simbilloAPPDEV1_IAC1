Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering

This program is a mix of computer science and electronic engineering. Under this program, students are trained with the handling of hardware and software for digital devices, designing and developing computers, and maintaining them.

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

This program focuses on the ins and outs of software engineering, programing, computing concepts, and algorithm development

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

A program for the foundation of developing professionals in the Information technology industry. This course teaches students on designing and implementing software or applications
